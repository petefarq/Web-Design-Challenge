Latitude vs. Humidity

Low Humidity at Tropics: Many of the lowest humidity cities were clustered with 10 degrees of 20 or -20 latitude. This is because our dataset pulled in cities in arid/desert regions that are clustered around those latitudes, including Iran, Namibia, Egypt, Mali, Algeria and central Australia. These areas receive air that has risen from moist equitorial regions, lost its moisture, and descended from the atmosphere as hot, dry air.
